The application allows to book a ticket from one place to desired destination. It shows you the bus layout and allows you to choose the seat. Afterwards the ticket is printed to pdf and confirmation is send to passengers e-mail address. Afterwards the ticket is added to MySQL database. The ticket table can managed / updated by mean of given graphical interface. Moreover you can add new routes to the program. Appropriate GUI is provided.
